Wapa Cafe UX Report

Client Vision

Client is a family-owned neighborhood restaurant. They have loyal clientele but are trying to expand their business to young professionals from nearby neighborhoods.

We chose to focus on the contact information, so first-time visitors could quickly find or contact Wapa, and pictures and design that invited and tempted them to visit.

They wanted to utilize graphic design materials they had already invested in, like their logo and menu design, but update the look and feel.

Keywords: Latin, lively, fresh food, happy hours, hip, cultural center.

Value Proposition

-Encourage user to visit in person or call to order by adding phone and contact details throughout the site.

-Make their mouths water. Lead with pictures, moving image background. Scroll-down section with more scrumptions images and text.

-Encourage user to inquire about events suggesting Wapa’s flexibility through an open-ended contact form.

-Convey the rustic and warm atmosphere of Wapa Cafe through inviting photos, text that tells a story, warm and rustic aesthetics.

-Integrate pre-existing logo, which had a Colonial feel in deep coffee colours and white.

Layout

Landing Page

-Logo, Social, Contact info, button to “Order now”. -Lead the user by scrolling down the main page to strips of professional pictures alternate with text about the cafe.

Contact

-Telephone number, location, and hours. -Google maps especially for mobile users.

Events

-Contact form. Encourage the user to describe their own vision for the event - we’re flexible! -Display events: artist showcases, networking, happy hours, private events.

Arepas

-Arepas, a very typical, fun Colombian food, are what set Wapa apart - separate menu.

Menu

-Leverage the existing PDF file and add warmth with a filter.
